About Galle

A City Where History
Meets the Indian Ocean

Galle is one of Sri Lanka's most captivating destinations — a southern coastal city where centuries of colonial history blend effortlessly with the natural beauty of tropical shorelines. Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Galle Fort remains one of the best-preserved examples of European architecture in South and Southeast Asia.

Located approximately 115 km south of Colombo, Galle is easily reached by train or bus. Within a 25 km radius lie beaches, temples, nature reserves, and authentic local dining experiences.
